What is the Riemann Hypothesis?

The Riemann Hypothesis is one of the most famous unsolved problems in mathematics. It deals with the zeros of the Riemann zeta function:

\[ \zeta(s) = \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{n^s} \]

The hypothesis states that all non-trivial zeros of the zeta function have real part equal to 1/2.
